## 2026-07-19 ‚Äî MS

Two normalisation algorithms are in use for gold‚Üísilver conversion (ry≈ç‚Üímonme, `normalization_method`): `ryo_to_monme@60_official1700`, the pegged official Tokugawa rate, and `ryo_to_monme@61_market_est`, an estimated market rate for periods where the official peg is known to have come under pressure. Where a transaction's rate is contested, both conversions are kept as separate rows (e.g. `CR-0002` vs `CR-0003`) rather than collapsed to one ‚Äî the discarded alternative stays in the data, flagged with lower `confidence`, so the sensitivity is auditable rather than buried in a footnote.
