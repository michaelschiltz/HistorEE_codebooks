# 2. Data inclusion and exclusion

> Record what enters the dataset and — more importantly — what is deliberately left out, and why.

## 2026-07-19 — MS

`instrument_type: bill_note` (手形 / tegata, `vocabularies/instrument_type.csv`) is coded as a single category across the sample period, but the term's referent broadens over the Edo period: early tegata read as a simple promissory note, later ones shade toward a negotiable instrument closer to `remittance_bill`. Kept as one code for now rather than split, because the sample is too thin to fix a period boundary between the two senses. Revisit once enough dated instances accumulate to locate where the shift falls; until then, `date_iso` lets a reader stratify by period and judge for themselves.
